Rental Process

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Choose Your Suit

Browse the collection and select the suit you want to rent.

Browse Suits

Tips for choosing the right suit for your event:

Consider the event's dress code

Think about the season and weather

Pick a color that complements your skin

Step 2: Get Measured

Explanation of the Fitting Process:

  • Accurate measurements ensure a perfect fit.
  • Our suits are tailored to your specific measurements.

Options for In-Store Fittings or Self-Measurement Guides:

  • Visit our store for a professional fitting.
  • Use our self-measurement guide at home.

Step 3: Place Your Order

How to Place an Order Online or In-Store:

  • Add your chosen suit to the cart and proceed to checkout.
  • Visit our store to place your order with the help of our staff.

Information on Providing Measurements and Selecting Rental Dates:

  • Enter your measurements online or provide them in-store.
  • Choose the rental period that fits your schedule.

Details on Any Required Deposits or Payments:

  • A deposit may be required to secure your rental.
  • Payment options include credit card, PayPal, and more.

Step 4: Receive Your Suit

Information on Delivery Options, Including Shipping or In-Store Pickup:

  • Select shipping to have the suit delivered to your address.
  • Choose in-store pickup to collect your suit personally.

Expected Delivery Times and Tracking Information:

  • Standard delivery takes 3-5 business days.
  • Tracking details will be provided via email.

Instructions for Trying on the Suit Upon Arrival to Ensure a Perfect Fit:

  • Try on the suit as soon as you receive it.
  • Contact us immediately if adjustments are needed.

Step 5: Enjoy Your Event

Reminders for Caring for the Suit During the Rental Period:

  • Avoid eating or drinking while wearing the suit to prevent stains.
  • Hang the suit when not in use to avoid wrinkles.

Tips for Looking Your Best and Making the Most of Your Rental:

  • Pair the suit with appropriate accessories.
  • Ensure shoes are polished and match the suit.

Step 6: Return the Suit

Instructions for returning the suit after the event:

  • Pack the suit in its original packaging.
  • Include all accessories and return forms.

Information on return options, including shipping or in-store drop-off:

  • Use the prepaid shipping label for returns.
  • Drop off the suit at our store.

Details on return deadlines and any potential late fees:

  • Return the suit within 2 days after the event.
  • Late fees may apply for delayed returns.
