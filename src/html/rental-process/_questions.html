Frequently Asked Questions

Find Answers to Common Rental Queries

How do I choose the right suit size?

Our Fit Guide provides detailed instructions on how to take accurate measurements. You can also visit our store for a professional fitting.

What is the rental period for a suit?

The standard rental period is 7 days, starting from the day you receive the suit. Extensions are available upon request.

What happens if the suit doesn't fit?

If the suit doesn't fit, contact us immediately. We offer free adjustments or a replacement suit to ensure you have the perfect fit for your event.
